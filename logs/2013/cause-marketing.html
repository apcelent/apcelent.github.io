Cause Marketing Gyaan and little more



It was a pleasure meeting Mr. Dhawal Udani, from Give India. He delivered a short lecture on cause marketing. No no, I am not going to write an essay on it, this wikipedia article does that. :-)

  • The business of business is a business. (=> meta-business = business ;-)
  • Cause Marketing is not marketing for a cause. It is marketing with a charitable component which relates the product to the cause.
  • Examples being - Shiksha from PNG, and Jago from TATA
  • MakeMyTrip has this initiative Go Green powered by GiveIndia

    • Reality Check

  • Don't start with Why it leads to over intellectualization.
  • Start with why not its more action driven.
  • The above, either you will figure out or market will tell you.
  • Everybody has plans, until they get punched in the face :-)
  • Market has a lot of entropy, that is why plans do *not* work, this entropy leads to serendipity.
  • Situational Intelligence is way better than analytical/quantitative/verbal (wish I understood it :P)
  • Good Business is Boring :-)
  • Its not about doing extraordinary things, nothing challenging conventions or ramboish, they are just cases of Inflection Points
